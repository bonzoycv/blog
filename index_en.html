The Beginning of an Adventure: My Journey into Game Creation

September 12, 2024

Today marks the beginning of an exciting personal adventure: the creation of my very own game. As someone with no prior experience in programming or digital art, this project represents an enormous challenge and, at the same time, the realization of a long-postponed dream.

Over the past few months, I've been working on the heart of my project: the story. The classic LucasArts graphic adventures have always been my inspiration, and now I'm setting out to create my own interactive narrative, filled with intriguing puzzles and memorable characters.

The Tools of My Adventure

  • Trello: To organize and manage project tasks.
  • Aseprite: My choice for creating characters and backgrounds in pixel art, capturing the essence of classic adventures.
  • Godot: The game engine I'll be using, chosen for its accessibility, open-source nature, and the abundance of learning resources available.

For now, my focus is on learning. I'm immersed in courses and tutorials about these tools, while continuing to develop the story and puzzles that will bring my game to life. I'm aware that the road will be long and challenging, but I'm determined to persevere.

This blog will be my development diary, a space where I'll share my progress, challenges, and learnings. I don't have a deadline for completing this project; my goal is to enjoy the process and, step by step, turn this dream into reality.

I hope you'll join me on this journey. Who knows? Maybe my experience will inspire others to dust off their own dreams and embark on their own creative adventures.

Let the adventure begin!

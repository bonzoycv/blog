About Me

Hello! I'm bonzoycv, a technology enthusiast with many years of experience using Linux operating systems, with a special weakness for distrohopping. My journey in the open-source world has been entirely self-taught, exploring and learning various tools along the way.

My passion for technology goes hand in hand with my love for adventure games, especially classic point and click graphic adventures. Titles like Monkey Island, Grim Fandango, The Dig, and Full Throttle have left an indelible mark on my imagination and creativity.

Besides video games, I'm an avid player of role-playing games like Dungeons and Dragons, where I can give free rein to my creativity and narrative skills. I also have a soft spot for B-movies, appreciating their unique charm and unrestricted creativity.

This blog is my way of combining all these passions into an exciting project: creating my own graphic adventure.
